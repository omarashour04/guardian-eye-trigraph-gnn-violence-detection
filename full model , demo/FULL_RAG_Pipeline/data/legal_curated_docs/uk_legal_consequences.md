## UK — Common assault and battery
Country: UK
Violent act: Common assault and battery
Keywords: UK, assault, battery, fight, physical contact, threat, violence
Legal meaning: Common assault and battery cover intentional or reckless unlawful force, or causing another person to apprehend immediate unlawful violence.
Possible legal consequence: A verified incident may lead to investigation or prosecution for common assault/battery. The court outcome depends on evidence, harm, aggravating factors, and sentencing rules.

Country: UK
Violent act: Assault occasioning actual bodily harm

## UK — Affray and public fighting
Country: UK
Violent act: Affray and public fighting
Keywords: UK, affray, public fighting, public order, violence, threat
